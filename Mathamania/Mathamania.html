About Mathamania

Do numbers fascinate you? Are you a Maths enthusiast? Did you enjoy those Maths classes in your school? Well, Mathmania brings you the perfect opportunity to revisit your school days and test how much of it do you still remember? Well, are you ready for the challenge?

Event Details:

A maximum of 80 participants will be allowed in the event on a first-come-first-serve basis.
The contestants will be given 30 minutes to solve the paper.


Rules:

1. The three winners of the contest will be decided based on the following parameters:
a) The contestants who come up with the correct solution the quickest.
b) If two or more contestants solve the question paper correctly simultaneously, another question paper will be provided as a tie-breaker. The maximum time allotted to solve that paper will be 10 minutes.
c) In case none of the contestants is able to solve the paper in the given time, the difference between the correct and incorrect entries will be calculated ( +1 for each correct entry, -1/2 for each incorrect one), and the contestant with the highest positive score will be declared the winner. If more than one participant has the highest positive score, the tie-breaker option will be availed. The rules mentioned in (b) will apply to the tie-breaker round.
2. Any disruptive or disorderly conduct from a participant will result in his/her immediate disqualification from the event.
3. In case of any conflict/controversy, the decision of the evaluators will be considered final and absolute.
4. Use black/blue ball-point pen only.


Team Details:

Mathamania is an Individual Event.


Eligibility:

All participants should have a valid College ID cards and be able to produce the same when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each player should be a student pursuing some field of study in Undergraduate courses only.


Registration Fees:

The one-time registration fee for each candidate is Rs.100/-.
