About

Game Version: NFS Most Wanted Black Edition v1.3
General Competition Method: Depending on the participant's number, 2-4 players per round.

Event Details

The Tournament is based on a knockout format.
A player who wins the 1st round advances to the next round.

Rules

1. The Tournament is based on a knockout format.
2. A player who wins the 1st round advances to the next round.
3.Courses: Circuit (Elimination rounds), Sprint (Final round).
4.Car: All the players will use a common car for fair play (like - VW GOLF GTI).
5.Race Mode Options:
   a.Nitrous: Default
   b.Collision Detection: Default
6.Players will be provided with a standard keyboard. Players may bring their keyboard or controller.
7.Visual Upgrade allowed.
8.Part, Performance (Pro, Super Pro, Ultimate)allowed, Junkman not allowed.
9.Personal saved files are NOT allowed.
10.Cars may be tuned before racing each course.
11.Unfair Play:
   a.Use of any cheat program
   b.Intentional disconnection
Any rule violation will disqualify a player from the Tournament.
N.B: Rules may subject to change during course of event.

Team Details & Registration Fees

Need For Speed will be an individual event and the entry fee will be Rs. 50 per participant along with the one-time registration fee of Rs. 100/-

Eligibility

All Participants should have proper College ID card on them, and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each participant should be a student pursuing some field of study in college/university.
