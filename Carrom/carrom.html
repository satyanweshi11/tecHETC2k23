About E-Carrom

E-Carrom is an Android game between two players.The rules of chess as we know them today emerged in Europe at the end of the 15th century, with standardization and universal acceptance by the end of the 19th century. Today, carrom is one of the world's most popular games, played by millions of people worldwide.


Rules:

• Opponent will be selected by lottery system.
• Playing with a new id (Guest id).
• Playing in a play carrom zone only.
• Mod/Hack version is not allowed.
• Both the players will have to use the same beginner(Striker,Power,pucks) and changing the striker or power will result in disqualification.
• Pucks Change is allowed.
• Leaving the game leads to loss.
• No comments/Massages shall be sent to opponent during the game.
• Any Unfair gameplay/discrepancy will result to disqualification.
• Decisions can be altered by coordinators if the requires so.
• All players must have enough coins (3k+) for the respective boards and Finals are conduct in(London park) board.
• Each player will be notified about their opponent just before the match and must add their Guest id before the conduction of match.
• The tournament is a knock-out one.
• All register players will be added to a whatsapp group,and winner of each game must share the winning screenshot in the group.
• Device or internet related issue will not be considered after the match starts.
